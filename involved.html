Volunteer Information

Hello and thank you for your interest in volunteering at our annual festival! We're excited to have community members like you who are enthusiastic about contributing to the success of this vibrant event. Volunteering at the festival is not only a great way to give back to the community but also an opportunity to experience the festivities from a unique perspective.

Volunteer Opportunities

We have a variety of roles available for volunteers, including:

  • Set-Up Crew: Assist with setting up booths, stages, and signage before the festival begins.
  • Information Booth Staff: Provide attendees with information about the festival, directions, and assistance.
  • Parking Attendants: Help manage parking areas and direct traffic.
  • Activity Assistants: Support in managing children's activities, arts and crafts areas, and other interactive zones.
  • Green Team: Assist with recycling efforts and maintaining cleanliness throughout the festival grounds.
  • Tear-Down Crew: Help dismantle and clean up after the festival concludes.

Benefits of Volunteering

As a volunteer, you'll receive:

  • A special volunteer T-shirt.
  • Complimentary snacks and beverages during your shift.
  • A certificate of appreciation for your contribution.
  • An opportunity to meet new people and be part of a community effort.

Requirements

To be a volunteer, you should:

  • Be at least 16 years old (volunteers under 18 require parental consent).
  • Commit to a minimum of a 4-hour shift.
  • Attend a brief training session prior to the festival.
  • Be friendly, enthusiastic, and willing to help.

How to Apply

To apply to be a volunteer:

Visit our festival website and fill out the volunteer application form. Choose your preferred role and availability. Submit the form, and we will contact you with further details.

Thank you once again for your interest in volunteering at our festival. Your support plays a crucial role in making this event a memorable experience for everyone involved. If you have any questions or need more information, please don't hesitate to reach out to our volunteer coordinator.

We can't wait to have you on our team!
